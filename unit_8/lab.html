The social and technological hub on campus!

The James Pedas Communication Center houses a 24 hour media lab, open to all students, faculty and staff. There are 24 computers available for use with a wide range of software capabilities, including Microsoft and Adobe products. The Pedas Center is also home to the Production Edit Suite. In this smaller lab, students can edit video and photos using industry standard software, such as Photoshop, Finalcut Pro X, Adobe Premier Pro and Avid.
