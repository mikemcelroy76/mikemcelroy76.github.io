WXTC

The studio of WXTC, home of Thiel College Radio, can be found inside the Pedas Center. The FCC licensed station is equipped with all new microphones, audio mixer, CD player, playlist controls, automated show rundowns, and remote broadcasting equipment. Using a staff of students, supervised by radio expert and adjunct professor, Peg Watts, WXTC caters to the college and local audience by airing hit music, gospel, reggae, and sports talk shows. WXTC Radio can be heard within a 25 mile radius of Thiel College's campus on 88.1 FM.

TCTV

The television studio and control room in the Pedas Center is home to the closed circuit, student run, campus television station, TCTV. The studio is also used for classroom media production instruction, as well as independent student productions. The studio is also home to the ISO Booth, used for voice and audio recording and editing. Equipment is available for students and faculty to check out and record stories and assignments out of the studio setting. If you're on Thiel's campus, tune in to channel 38 to check out TCTV.
